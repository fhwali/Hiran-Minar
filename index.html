Hiran Minar Tomb

Hiran Minar is an early 17th-century Mughal era complex located in Sheikhupura, in the Pakistani province of Punjab.

The complex was built at the site of a game reserve in honour of Emperor Jahangir's beloved antelope named Mansraj.
